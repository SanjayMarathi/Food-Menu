How to Use FoodApp

FoodApp is a digital restaurant system for QR-based ordering, live kitchen tracking, and staff-friendly management.

For Customers For Staff QR Setup FAQ

Overview

FoodApp streamlines dining with a QR-powered menu and a real-time staff dashboard. Customers scan the table QR, browse the menu, add items to the cart, and place an order. The kitchen sees live orders; staff update statuses (Pending ‚Üí Preparing ‚Üí Completed), and admins manage items, revenue, and QR codes.

For Customers

  1. Scan the QR on your table to open the digital menu.
  2. Browse items; tap a card to view description; tap + Add to Cart.
  3. Open the cart, review items, and tap Place Order.
  4. Your order becomes Pending for the kitchen; wait for staff for billing.

For Staff & Admin

Kitchen Flow

  • Open Kitchen & Revenue Dashboard.
  • See Active Orders; update status: Pending ‚Üí Preparing ‚Üí Completed/Cancelled.
  • Mark completed and collect payment.

Menu Management

  • Add/Edit/Delete items: name, price, description, image.
  • See Top Selling Items and total revenue.
  • Generate and print QR Codes for tables.

QR Code Setup

  1. Go to Staff Dashboard ‚Üí Generate Table QR Codes.
  2. Enter table number ‚Üí click Generate & Print.
  3. Place the printed QR on the physical table.
  4. When scanned, customers open the menu pre-filled with the table number.

Tip: laminate the print for durability.
